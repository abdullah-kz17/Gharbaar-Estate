AI-Powered Real Estate Platform for Buying, Selling & Renovation

The Problem

Pakistan's real estate market lacks transparency, reliable renovation services, and personalized property search, leading to customer dissatisfaction and inefficient transactions.

Our Solution

An integrated platform combining AI-powered property search, automated renovation assessment, and expert connectivity for seamless real estate transactions.

AI-Powered Features

Smart Property Search

Natural language search using AI prompts. Describe your dream home in plain English and get personalized recommendations.

AI Renovation Assessment

Computer vision models analyze property images to automatically detect renovation needs and provide instant recommendations.

Expert Matching

Location-based contractor recommendations with ratings, reviews, and real-time availability for seamless service connections.

Price Prediction

ML-powered price estimation considering market trends, location factors, and property conditions for accurate valuations.
